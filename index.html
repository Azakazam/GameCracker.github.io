The Nintendo Switch

The Nintendo Switch system is a games console created by the Nintendo that can transform to suit your situation, so you can play the games you want, no matter how busy life may be. It's an era where you don't have to adjust your lifestyle to play games - instead, your console adjusts to fit around your lifestyle. Enjoy games anytime, anywhere, with anyone, with flexible and free play modes.

Should I buy a Nintendo Switch?


Five reasons why the Nintendo Switch is a must buy console:
  • The console features three playable modes: TV, table-top and handheld.
  • You can take screenshots and videos of the games you play and share them on social media
  • The switch comes at an affordable price compared to other consoles.
  • It has a wide range of games to choose from including: online, offline and multiplayer type games.
  • The console comes with multiple accessories allowing you to cuztomize the switch to whatever appeals to you.
